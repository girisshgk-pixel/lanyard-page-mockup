Product Features

Six Reasons These Lanyards Deliver Superior Results

Every detail is designed to maximise brand visibility, ensure comfort, and guarantee a professional finish that holds up long-term.

Vibrant Silk Screen Printing

High-quality silk screen printing delivers sharp logos and bold colours that remain clear and eye-catching, even with frequent daily use. Your brand always looks its best.

Durable Polyester Construction

Made from strong polyester fibres, these branded lanyards are designed to withstand daily wear while maintaining their shape and professional appearance over time.

Single & Double-Sided Printing

Choose single-sided for cost efficiency or double-sided for maximum brand exposure on both faces — ideal for environments where the lanyard rotates during wear.

Multiple Width Options

Choose from 1cm, 1.5cm, 2cm, and 2.5cm widths to balance comfort, branding impact, and budget. Wider lanyards offer more surface area for logos and text.

Customisable Attachments

Select from bulldog clips, safety breakaway clips, split rings, swivel clasps, or retractable reels — perfectly matched to your organisation's safety and operational needs.

Standard 90cm Length

All lanyards arrive in the industry-standard 90cm length — universally comfortable for all-day wear and compatible with ID holders, access cards, keys, and reels.

Single-sided printing applies your design to one face of the lanyard — clean, cost-effective, and suitable for most corporate and event uses. Double-sided printing applies your design to both faces, ensuring your brand is visible regardless of how the lanyard hangs or rotates during wear.
